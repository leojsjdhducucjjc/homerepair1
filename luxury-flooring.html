Flooring FAQs

What is best for lake homes?

Waterproof vinyl plank is the most common for moisture resistance and easy maintenance.

Can you install over existing floors?

Sometimes yes, depending on flatness and condition. We confirm after onsite review.

Do you help with product selection?

Yes. We bring finish samples and recommend options by budget and lifestyle.
