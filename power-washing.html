Power Washing FAQs

Will pressure washing damage wood?

No. We dial pressure by material and age to avoid gouging or splintering.

How often should lake homes be cleaned?

Most exterior surfaces benefit from annual cleaning; high-shade areas may need more.

Do you clean docks before staining?

Yes, cleaning prep is one of the most common steps before refinishing.
