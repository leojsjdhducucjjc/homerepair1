Spring Maintenance Checklist for Lake Homes

Getting ahead of seasonal upkeep can save money and reduce stress once lake traffic picks up.

Spring is a good time to inspect the property before the busy season starts. Catching small issues early often helps owners avoid bigger repairs later.

Check Paint and Trim

Look for scuffs, peeling, fading, or moisture-related wear. Freshening high-visibility areas can make the property feel instantly cleaner.

Inspect Floors and Entry Areas

Entryways and common areas usually take the most abuse. If flooring is worn or damaged, early replacement can help before guests start arriving regularly.

Wash Exterior Surfaces

Docks, decks, siding, walkways, and patios often benefit from a good cleaning after winter. Removing buildup also helps you see what may need repair or refinishing.
