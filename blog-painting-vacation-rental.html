Painting Tips for Vacation Rental Homes at the Lake

Rental homes need finishes that photograph well, clean easily, and survive busy guest turnover.

Updating, and keeping visually appealing a vacation rental requires a considerable amount of time and money for its’ management, but there are a lot of simple tricks to keep your property looking current without eating into your bottom line. One trick for your property to have a fresh updated look is to paint.

Use Colors That Feel Clean and Bright

When choosing colors for your vacation rental listing images, stick with soft whites, warm neutrals and natural colors. These options will likely look the best to the broadest audience.

Choose Finishes That Can Be Wiped Down

Your interior will see more use than usual as you rent out your property, meaning rental traffic will leave more scratches on your luggage, and more smudges on your surfaces. Choosing more washable finishes will hopefully reduce the damage.

Plan Around Turnover Windows

It's usually better to have a painting job completed out of season to avoid losing rental income. To complete a painting job to the highest standard, there must be ample time allocated to the project.
